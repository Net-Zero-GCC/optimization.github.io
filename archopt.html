Workflow

Four steps from IDF to optimal design

The full simulation-optimization pipeline inside one desktop application.

01

Define variables

Set design parameters — WWR, shading depth, glazing type, orientation — with ranges and constraints in the GUI.

02

EnergyPlus sampling

Latin Hypercube Sampling generates diverse design combinations. Each is automatically simulated in EnergyPlus.

03

Surrogate training

XGBoost learns from simulation data and builds a fast surrogate model, evaluating thousands of candidates in seconds.

04

Pareto front export

NSGA-III finds the optimal trade-off surface. Full archive, CSV data, and PDF report exported automatically.

Can I move my license to a new computer?

Each key activates on one machine. If you replace your computer, email architecture.optimization@gmail.com with your license key and the license can be reset for activation on the new machine.

What happens after one year?

The license is valid for one year from activation. After expiry, the software prompts for a new key — renewing is the same one-time purchase, with no automatic billing.

Only briefly: once for activation, and occasionally to re-verify the license. All simulations and optimizations run entirely on your own machine — your models never leave your computer.

No. ARCHOPT is a desktop application with a full graphical interface. You define variables, ranges, and objectives in the GUI; the EnergyPlus runs, surrogate training, and NSGA-III optimization are fully automated.
