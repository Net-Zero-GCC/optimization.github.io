Common questions
What happens when my plan expires? +
As long as your Gumroad subscription is active, your key stays valid. If you cancel or a payment fails, ARCHOPT will show a renewal screen — resubscribe and your key reactivates.
Can I switch from monthly to yearly? +
Yes — cancel your monthly subscription on Gumroad, then subscribe to the yearly plan. Email us if you'd like help moving your key over.
Can I use the license on more than one computer? +
Each license is bound to a single machine. If you change computers, contact us and we'll reset your license for free.
How quickly will I receive my key? +
Your key is emailed right after your payment is confirmed. In rare cases it may take a few hours — if so, just email us and we'll send it manually.
Can I get a refund? +
We offer a 7-day refund if ARCHOPT does not work on your machine and we are unable to fix it. Contact us by email within 7 days of purchase.
