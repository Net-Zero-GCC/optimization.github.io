Workflow

Four steps from IDF to optimal design

The full simulation-optimization pipeline inside one desktop application. No coding required.

01

Define variables

Set design parameters - WWR, shading depth, glazing type, orientation - with ranges and constraints in the GUI.

02

EnergyPlus sampling

Latin Hypercube Sampling generates diverse design combinations. Each is automatically simulated in EnergyPlus.

03

Surrogate training

04

Pareto front export

NSGA-III finds the optimal trade-off surface. Full archive, CSV data, and PDF report exported automatically.

Capabilities

Everything you need, in one pipeline

Multi-objective NSGA-III

Optimize energy, embodied carbon, and comfort simultaneously. Handles 3+ objectives without compromising solution diversity.

Full IDF parametric control

XGBoost surrogate model

60-year lifecycle analysis

Scale energy (kWh/m²) and carbon results over the full building lifetime. Floor area and study period are user-configurable.

Embodied carbon calculator

Ajman University - Research Grant
Assessing overheating vulnerability in desert-climate school buildings under current and future climate scenarios (2020–2090). Calibrating building simulation models against measured indoor temperature data and applying multi-objective genetic algorithms to assess and mitigate overheating risk in existing Canadian schools under extreme current and future climates.
